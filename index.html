PRESENT SIMPLE

Truths, habits, routines, facts and fixed situations
1. WHAT IS IT?

The Present Simple is used to talk about actions that are regular, true or always happen.

📅every day
🕒usually
🔁often
📌on Mondays
❌never

3. FORM
Subject Affirmative Negative Question
I / You / We / They I play. I don’t play. Do I play?
He / She / It He plays. He doesn’t play. Does he play?

7. COMMON MISTAKES
  • ❌ He go to school. → ✅ He goes to school.
  • ❌ She don’t like coffee. → ✅ She doesn’t like coffee.
  • ❌ Do they goes? → ✅ Do they go?
