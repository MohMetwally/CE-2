📘 Present Simple

Use it. Understand it. Master it.

📌 1. What is it?

The Present Simple is used to talk about actions that are regular, true or always happen.

🗓️ always — usually — often — sometimes — rarely — never — every day — on Mondays

🌍 2. Uses

Habits

I brush my teeth every morning.

General Truths

Water boils at 100°C.

Facts

Bananas grow in tropical countries.

Schedules

The bus leaves at 8 o’clock.

Feelings

I love chocolate.

✏️ 3. Form
Subject Affirmative Negative Question
I / You / We / They I play football. I don’t play football. Do I play football?
He / She / It He plays football. He doesn’t play football. Does he play football?
⭐ 4. Spelling Rules
  • play → plays
  • watch → watches
  • study → studies
  • enjoy → enjoys
📊 5. Adverbs of Frequency
🔴 100% always
🟠 80% usually
🟡 60% often
🟢 40% sometimes
🔵 20% rarely
🟣 0% never
⏰ 6. Time Expressions
  • 📅 every day / week / month
  • 🕒 once a week
  • 🌞 in the morning
  • 🌙 at night
  • 📌 on Mondays
❌ 7. Common Mistakes
  • ❌ He go to school. → ✅ He goes to school.
  • ❌ She don’t like coffee. → ✅ She doesn’t like coffee.
  • ❌ Do they goes? → ✅ Do they go?
