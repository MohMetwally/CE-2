PRESENT SIMPLE

Truths, habits, routines, facts and fixed situations

1. WHAT IS IT?

The Present Simple is used to talk about actions that are regular, true or always happen.

Keywords:

always, usually, often, sometimes, rarely, never, every day/week/month, once a week, on Mondays, etc.

2. USES
🪥

Habits & Routines

I brush my teeth every morning.

🌍

General Truths

Water boils at 100°C.

📖

Facts

Bananas grow in tropical countries.

🚌

Schedules

The bus leaves at 8 o’clock.

❤️

Feelings & States

I love chocolate.

3. FORM
Subject Affirmative (+) Negative (-) Question (?)
I / You / We / They I play football. I do not / don’t play football. Do I play football?
He / She / It He plays football. He does not / doesn’t play football. Does he play football?
⭐ He / She / It → add -s / -es to the verb

play → plays   watch → watches   go → goes
study → studies   have → has
⚙️ Spelling Rules
  • Most verbs: add -s
  • -s, -sh, -ch, -x, -o → add -es
  • consonant + y → change y to i + es
  • vowel + y → add -s

5. TIME EXPRESSIONS
  • ⏰ every day / week / month / year
  • 📅 once a week / twice a month
  • 🕒 on Mondays / in the morning
  • ☀️ at night / at the weekend
  • 🕰️ in the summer / in the evening
6. EXAMPLES BY LEVEL

⭐ Beginner

  • I live in Cairo.
  • She has a cat.
  • They play football.
  • The sun rises in the east.

📖 Elementary

  • He works in a bank.
  • I usually walk to school.
  • They don’t watch TV.
  • Does she like coffee?

📈 Intermediate

  • I prefer tea to coffee.
  • She doesn’t like spicy food.
  • Do they often travel abroad?
  • He goes jogging every morning.

🏆 Advanced

  • Society relies on rules.
  • Technology changes rapidly.
  • She consistently shows dedication.
  • Do you believe success comes from hard work?
7. COMMON MISTAKES
  • ❌ He go to school every day. → ✅ He goes to school every day.
  • ❌ She don’t like ice cream. → ✅ She doesn’t like ice cream.
  • ❌ Do they goes to the park? → ✅ Do they go to the park?
  • ❌ I always get up late. → ✅ I always get up late.
